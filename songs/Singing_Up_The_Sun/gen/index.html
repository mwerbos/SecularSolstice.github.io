Singing Up The Sun

By Ben Newman

The point of a solstice celebration

Original chords (in a somewhat confusing format) are here and original recording is here.

This song does a great job of linking modern and ancient solstice celebrations. It does have a few potential issues as regards epistemic virtue. The whole “we can say” thing feels a little motte-bailey-ish and the “have to believe” definitely isn’t how we do things. So maybe follow this with a Litany of Tarski over whether the change we make is for real or not.
