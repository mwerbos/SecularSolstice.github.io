We Are The Light

By Logan Strohl

This dark, cold, terrifying world is ours to conquer

This song is a relyricization of Ave Verum Corpus by Mozart. New lyrics written by Logan Strohl.

A Bayesian Choir Performance of this song was on Youtube but has been deleted.

There is sheet music available for this song. There's also a recording from the DC Solstice 2023 band.
