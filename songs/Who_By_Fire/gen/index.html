Who By Fire

By Leonard Cohen

Who will pass on in the coming year? How? And, perhaps, why?

Based on the much older (12th century? 9th century?) Unetanah tokef. But removing the framing about writing and sealing the Book of Life, so that examples become questions. The song is filled with ominous uncertainty.

A performance by Leonard Cohen is on youtube.
