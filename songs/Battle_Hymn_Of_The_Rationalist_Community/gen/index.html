Battle Hymn Of The Rationalist Community

By Scott Alexandar (Lyrics)

A triumphant celebration of truth and humanity

To the tune of The Battle Hymn of the Republic.

Pieces of this first appeared as The Battle Hymn of the Republic of Letters as part of the Fermat’s Last Stand Dungeons and Discourses campaign.
